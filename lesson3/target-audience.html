Target Audience

Almost everyone is concerned with the weather; many people need to plan outdoor events and check the weather often. This site will target all those who spend time outside. Adults are more likely to check the weather than children, so they are also a target demographic. Staying up to date on how the weather will turn out is also an important thing for anyone planning an event.

Possible Scenarios

  • A baseball coach needs to know if it will rain over the weekend so he'll know whether or not to cancel the game.

  • A parent is planning a family camping trip and needs to know where and when the best weather will happen.

  • Someone may wonder how many more days it will be before it stops snowing.

  • A homeowner may check the weather to see if they should water their outdoor plans and lawn or not.

  • Gardeners may also check the weather to see if it will be a good day to plant.
