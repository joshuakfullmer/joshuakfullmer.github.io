Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Dollar Shave Club

This is a good example of Visual Hierarchy . The page is simplified for moblie and the biggest button is bright orange and grabs the users attention right away.

White Space

Electronic Arts

The use of White Space on EA's site helps draw the user's attention to the article headlines. White space also helps separate the different articles.

Law of Proximity

Twitter

Twitter's moblie site is a great example of the Law of Proximity. Different tweats are seperated with alternating white and grey backgrounds that help group information.
